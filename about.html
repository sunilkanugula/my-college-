Welcome to SVCET:(Code: VETS)

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering & Technology ETCHERLA was established in 2008 by VIKAS EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY(1997) with an avowed objective of accelerating the growth of science and technology in this industrially backward Srikakulam district. The Institution aims at providing state-of-the-art technology to the student community to broaden their horizon for a better and prospective tomorrow in their own interest and also in interest of the society at large. We have extensive record in the field of education. The institution is endowed with highly qualified and seasoned faculty on its rolls. Sri VENKATESWARA College of Engineering & Technology is committed to “impart quality education to the youth enable them the right attitude, professional competancy and inculcate ethical values”.

With a clear mission to faster the advancement of science and technology in the industrially underdeveloped Srikakulam district. Our primary goal is to equip students with state-of-the-art technology, expanding their knowledge for a brighter and promising future, benefiting both their personal growth and the welfare of society. With a remarkable track record in education, our institution boasts a highly qualified and experienced faculty. At Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering & Technology, we are dedicated to imparting quality education to our youth, nurturing the right attitude, developing professional competency, and instilling ethical values in our students. We strive to empower them to make a positive impact in the world."

Departments :

CSE

Computer Science and Engineering
A field of study and profession that focuses on the design, development, and application of computer systems, software, and hardware to solve real-world problems and advance technology.

AIML

Arificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
Utilizes algorithms and data to enable machines to learn and make intelligent decisions, driving advancements in artificial intelligence applications.

Mechanical

Mechanical
Involves the design and development of mechanical systems, applying principles of physics and materials science to various industries, from transportation to energy and beyond.

ECE

Electronics and Communication Engineering
Focuses on the design, analysis, and application of electronic and communication systems, enabling advancements in telecommunications, signal processing, and embedded technologies.

EEE

Electrical and Electronics Engineering
Deals with the study, design, and application of electrical and electronic systems, including power generation, distribution, electronics, and control systems, to address various societal and industrial needs.

Civil

Civil Engineering
Involves the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure and buildings, such as roads, bridges, dams, and skyscrapers, contributing to the development and modernization of communities and societies.
