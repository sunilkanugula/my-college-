Departments :

CSE

Computer Science and Engineering
A field of study and profession that focuses on the design, development, and application of computer systems, software, and hardware to solve real-world problems and advance technology.

AIML

Arificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
Utilizes algorithms and data to enable machines to learn and make intelligent decisions, driving advancements in artificial intelligence applications.

Mechanical

Mechanical
Involves the design and development of mechanical systems, applying principles of physics and materials science to various industries, from transportation to energy and beyond.

ECE

Electronics and Communication Engineering
Focuses on the design, analysis, and application of electronic and communication systems, enabling advancements in telecommunications, signal processing, and embedded technologies.

EEE

Electrical and Electronics Engineering
Deals with the study, design, and application of electrical and electronic systems, including power generation, distribution, electronics, and control systems, to address various societal and industrial needs.

Civil

Civil Engineering
Involves the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure and buildings, such as roads, bridges, dams, and skyscrapers, contributing to the development and modernization of communities and societies.
