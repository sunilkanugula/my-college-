Laboratories :

Our college takes immense pride in offering an array of state-of-the-art laboratories tailored for mechanical engineering students. These labs provide hands-on experiences to complement theoretical knowledge. The Materials Testing Lab offers insights into material properties, while the Thermal Sciences Lab delves into thermodynamics and heat transfer. In the Dynamics and Kinematics Lab, students explore motion principles, CAD/CAM Lab focuses on design and manufacturing, and the Fluid Mechanics Lab examines fluid behavior. The Robotics and Automation Lab covers robotics and programming, and the Mechanical Vibration Lab studies mechanical oscillations. Our well-equipped Mechanical Workshop hones practical skills. These labs collectively prepare students to excel as skilled mechanical engineers in a rapidly evolving technological world.
